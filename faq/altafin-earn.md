# AltaFin Earn

**How to change the current Network being used?** To switch between networks, go to your wallet and select the network that you want to use. Once you make that change in your wallet, the website will get updated and you will see the name of the network by your wallet address in the website's header.

**What Networks do you support?** We currently support the Ethereum Mainnet, Polygon, and Ropsten.

**How do I switch wallets?** To switch wallets, disconnect your current wallet from the connection. That will reload the website and show the "Connect to Wallet" button.

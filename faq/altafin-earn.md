# Alta Finance Earn

## **Why Earn?**

Earn makes it easy to have exposure to the underlying real-world asset revenue streams wrapped in a smart contract.

## **How does Alta Finance Earn work?**

Earn runs natively on Ethereum and Polygon (other chains launching soon) and is powered by ERC-20 infrastructure. All Earn contracts are opened with our native ALTA token, which can easily be acquired inside your MetaMask, Coinbase, or Web3 wallet.

**How will my principal be returned?**\
****All contracts are quoted in USDC, and the principal will be returned at contract close in USDC.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## **What if I need to close my position early?**

We have created a market to re-sell Earn contracts to 3rd parties, allowing any owner to exit their position at any time. To accomplish this, place your contract For Sale by clicking the "Sell Contract" button in the top right corner of your contract and accept the highest bid on your terms.

## **How to change the current Network being used?**&#x20;

To switch between networks, go to your wallet and select the network that you want to use. Once you make that change in your wallet, the website will get updated and you will see the name of the network by your wallet address in the website's header.

## **What Networks do you support?**&#x20;

We currently support the Ethereum Mainnet, Polygon, and Ropsten.

## **How do I switch wallets?**&#x20;

To switch wallets, disconnect your current wallet from the connection. That will reload the website and show the "Connect to Wallet" button.

## **Is Alta Finance Earn open to everyone or only accredited investors?**

Earn is open for everyone but US citizens due to the current regulatory environment.

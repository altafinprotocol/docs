**Will Real Estate tenants/owners have to pay in Alta Token for leases and fees?**\
****No, Real Estate at the start will be in fiat until the industry catches up. This is because we plan on owning leveraged real estate (for example, on a $100m building, we will carry $75m of debt) which provides better use of capital and debt paydown over time. So, we would need to service and denominate these transactions in fiat USD using the LLC's that own them.\
But we aim to act as the bridge between the two using our platform, treasury, and processes. We have some exciting plans when the industry does get there, but no set timeline.

**What is the Dream Fund?**\
****It is the fund that we will use to design, develop, and launch innovative real estate products globally.

# xAFN Staking

**Where and how can I stake my AFN for xAFN?** Here is a detailed guide con how to stake: [How to Stake AFN for xAFN](../tutorials/how-to-stake-afn-for-xafn.md)

**If I stake my xAFN on another platform do I continue to earn AFN rewards?** Yes, you do. Your AFN rewards will continue to accrue. You can view your rewards here: [app.altafin.co/stake](https://app.altafin.co/stake).

**What is the current ROI for xAFN staking?** You can check the ROI for xAFN here: [app.altafin.co/stake](xafn-staking.md#faq). Please note that this number changes continuously due to fluctuating volume on our platform and the number of AFN staked. It, therefore, is difficult for us to present future predictions on its development.

**Why is my xAFN balance lower than my AFN balance?** When you stake your AFN tokens, you “purchase” a share of the xAFN pool. Because xAFN appreciates in value compared to AFN, the amount of xAFN you get for your AFN decreases overtime. ****&#x20;

**Where do staking rewards come from?** From platform fees and AltaFin Real Estate Profits.

**How to change the current Network being used?** To switch between networks, go to your wallet and select the network that you want to use. Once you make that change in your wallet, the website will get updated and you will see the name of the network by your wallet address in the website's header.

**What Networks do you support?** We currently support the Ethereum Mainnet, Ropsten, Goerli, and Kovan Test Networks.

**How do I switch wallets?** To switch wallets, disconnect your current wallet from the connection. That will reload the website and show the "Connect to Wallet" button.

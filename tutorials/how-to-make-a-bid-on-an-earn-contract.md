# How to make a bid on an Earn contract

1\. Go to [https://app.altafin.co/earn](https://app.altafin.co/earn)

2\. Click For Sale

3\. Click on the contract that you want to make a bid on

4\. Click Make Bid

![](<../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2022-02-08 at 4.06.41 PM.png>)

5\. Enter the amount of AFN that you want to offer

6\. Click Make Bid

![](../.gitbook/assets/FE133FE4-166B-4731-B060-8495268468A6.jpeg)

7\. Approve the transaction in your wallet

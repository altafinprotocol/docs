# How to get Alta Tokens through Metamask

1. Log in to Metamask

![](<../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2022-04-13 at 2.10.17 PM (1).png>)

2\. Click Swap

![](<../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2022-04-13 at 2.10.48 PM.png>)

3\. Choose the token that you want to swap for Alta Finance Tokens

![](<../.gitbook/assets/Screen Shot 2022-04-13 at 2.12.21 PM.png>)

4\. Click Review Swap and then Swap

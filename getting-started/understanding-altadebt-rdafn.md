# Understanding AltaDebt (rdALTA)

Alta Debt was created by Alta Finance to represent any real-world liabilities (debt) that Alta Finance is obligated to for real estate including private loans, mortgages, and seller-financing or seller carry. The Alta Finance Treasury wallet is the sole holder of this token.

The sole intended purpose of this token is for public transparency of Alta Finance treasury funds. 1 rdALTA represents $1 of Alta Finance real world debts.

Alta Finance will publicly announce anytime we conduct a mint/burn.

#### Mint

We will only mint when we acquire debt for the purchase of an asset.

#### Burn

We will only burn as we pay down debt either according to our regularly scheduled debt obligations or as we complete projects.

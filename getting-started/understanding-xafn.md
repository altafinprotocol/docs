# Understanding xAFN

xAFN is the token that you receive in exchange for staking AFN tokens. To learn more staking, go to [How to Stake AFN for xAFN](../tutorials/how-to-stake-afn-for-xafn.md).\
\
The xAFN token is continuously compounding accruing value from real estate dividends and platform fees, hence it will always be worth more than a regular AFN token.\


In the future, xAFN will give you governance benefits, meaning that it will give you voting rights on the addition and removal of lending assets on the AltaFin Platform and on the addition, removal, and adjustments to stake rates and loyalty bonuses.

\
Stake your AFN for xAFN here: [https://app.altafin.co/stake](https://app.altafin.co/stake)\
\
If you want to have an idea of the current APY, you can check it out here: [https://app.altafin.co/stake](https://app.altafin.co/stake)

# Understanding Alta Asset (raALTA)

Alta Asset was created by Alta Finance to represent any real-world assets that Alta Finance owns, including land, commercial buildings, multi-family apartments, and condos. The Alta Finance Treasury wallet is the sole holder of this token.

The sole intended purpose of this token is for public transparency of Alta Finance treasury funds. 1 raALTA represents $1 of Alta Finance real world assets.

Alta Finance will publicly announce anytime we conduct a mint/burn.

#### Mint

We will only mint when we acquire an asset or conduct a net asset valuation and adjust the portfolio accordingly.

#### Burn

We will only burn when we dispose of an asset or conduct a net asset valuation and adjust the portfolio accordingly.

# Understanding AFN

The AltaFin AFN Token powers the entire ecosystem of current and future AltaFin products, smart contracts, and governance.\
\
AFN is currently issued on Ethereum following ERC-20 standard. The token's contract address is: 0xe46f290cd59195a83e757891430d8d517d16b334\


### **Token Utility**

#### **Staking**

* AFN is accepted as a staking asset to increase loyalty rewards (interest rate bonuses)
* AFN portfolio holders receive an interest bonus on AltaFin Earn Contracts
* Interest bonus will be converted to AFN before being distributed
* Long-term AFN holders will accumulate more voting power for governance

The AFN token is now available for instant swap via AMM's like SushiSwap® or Uniswap® instantly in your Web3 wallet.

Currently, earn an AFN bonus on Earn contracts. Paid out instantly at contract close date.
